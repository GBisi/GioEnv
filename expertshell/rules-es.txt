IF temperature(very_low) DO set_temperature(high).
IF temperature(low) and outdoor_temperature(very_low) DO set_temperature(high).
IF temperature(high) and outdoor_temperature(very_high) DO set_temperature(low).
IF temperature(very_high) DO set_temperature(low).

IF light(low) and outdoor_light(medium) DO set_light(medium).
IF light(low) and outdoor_light(low) DO set_light(high).
IF light(medium) and outdoor_light(low) DO set_light(high).
IF light(medium) and outdoor_light(high) DO set_light(medium).
IF light(high) and outdoor_light(high) DO set_light(medium).
IF light(high) and outdoor_light(medium) DO set_light(medium).
